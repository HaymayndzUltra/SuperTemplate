## üìã PRODUCTION NOTES

### Technical Specifications
- **Resolution:** 4K (3840x2160) minimum for professional quality
- **Aspect Ratio:** 16:9 standard
- **Frame Rate:** 30fps (smooth motion) or 60fps (for screen recordings)
- **Audio:** Professional voiceover with background music (subtle, modern tech vibe)
- **Subtitles:** Optional but recommended for accessibility

### Visual Style Guide
- **Color Palette:**
  - Primary: Deep Blue (#1E3A8A) - Trust, professionalism
  - Accent: Teal/Cyan (#06B6D4) - Innovation, technology
  - Background: Dark Gray (#111827) - Modern, sleek
  - Highlights: Gold (#F59E0B) - Premium, excellence
  
- **Typography:**
  - Headers: Bold, modern sans-serif (e.g., Inter Bold, Montserrat Bold)
  - Body: Clean, readable sans-serif (e.g., Inter Regular, Roboto)
  - Code/Technical: Monospace (e.g., Fira Code, JetBrains Mono)

- **Animation Style:**
  - Smooth, professional transitions (ease-in-out curves)
  - Subtle motion graphics (not distracting)
  - Consistent timing (0.3s transitions standard)
  - Professional bounce/elastic effects avoided

### Screen Recording Best Practices
- **Clean Desktop:** Remove personal info, use neutral background
- **Cursor Highlighting:** Animated cursor trail for visibility
- **Zoom Effects:** Strategic zoom-ins on important details
- **Highlighting:** Use colored overlays to emphasize key elements
- **Smooth Scrolling:** Record at normal speed, adjust in post

### Audio Recommendations
- **Voiceover:**
  - Professional male/female voice (Upwork voice talent)
  - Clear articulation, confident but not aggressive
  - Pace: 150-160 words per minute
  - Tone: Warm, professional, slightly enthusiastic
  
- **Background Music:**
  - Genre: Upbeat tech/startup music
  - Volume: 20-30% of voiceover level
  - No lyrics during voiceover
  - Fade in/out smoothly

### Editing Guidelines
- **Pacing:** Keep energy high, no dead air
- **Transitions:** Use consistent transition style throughout
- **On-Screen Text:** Large, readable, on-screen for 3-4 seconds minimum
- **Key Moments:** Slow down slightly for important demonstrations
- **B-Roll:** Use screen recordings, graphics, and animations to maintain visual interest
