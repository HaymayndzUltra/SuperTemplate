## üé® VISUAL STYLE CONSISTENCY

### Color Palette
- **Primary Blue:** #1E3A8A (Trust, professionalism)
- **Accent Teal:** #06B6D4 (Innovation, technology)
- **Background Dark:** #111827 (Modern, sleek)
- **Highlights Gold:** #F59E0B (Premium, excellence)
- **Success Green:** #10B981 (Positive outcomes)
- **Warning Red:** #EF4444 (Attention, but not alarm)

### Typography Hierarchy
1. **Primary Headers:** 48-60px, Bold, Blue/Teal
2. **Secondary Headers:** 36-42px, Semi-Bold, White/Light Gray
3. **Body Text:** 24-28px, Regular, White/Light Gray
4. **Metrics:** 72-96px, Bold, Gold/Teal
5. **Labels:** 18-20px, Medium, Gray

### Animation Principles
- **Timing:** 0.3s standard transitions, 0.5s for emphasis
- **Easing:** Ease-in-out for professional feel
- **Scale:** Subtle (105-110%) for emphasis
- **Glow:** Gentle pulsing (1.5s cycle)
- **Fade:** Smooth alpha transitions

### Visual Effects
- **Subtle Motion:** Background gradients animate slowly
- **Depth:** Light shadows and glows for dimension
- **Focus:** Strategic blur on non-essential elements
- **Highlighting:** Colored overlays for emphasis
- **Polish:** Consistent rounded corners (8-12px radius)
